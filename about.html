"We wanted to set up a kennels where we would be happy to leave our Frenchie."

We wanted to set up a kennels where I would be happy to leave my dog. I believed that dog psychology approach helps to keep our guests calm and quiet in an environment that would not stress them, helping and caring about them in whatever needs and difficulty they might forced to face. Over the years our client base has grown at an incredible rate and we have over 4,000 dogs on our books from all over the UK. Our clients have really embraced our approach, helping to grow and build a fantastic reputation. Finally, as we know that this is an extremely hard time for you and your pet, we offer a specific assistance in our centre which support you, everytime of the year, during the process, helping you going through this tough time.

Welcome to Buddy Care Centre

My name is Federico Calzati.
I have created Buddy Care Centre to offers practical, real-life support and training advice to help you care for your indisposed best friend.

I have experienced my French Bulldog named Alba,who suddenly stopped walking. After visiting the emergency vet at the Royal Veterinary College Hospital in London, they performed an MRI scan, and the the situation was clear. Alba suffered a 4thstage IVDD with her 50% chance to get her back on running.

However, since the accident, she remained paraplegyc, therefore incontinent, not being able to control her bladder and colon.

With over 2 years of experience caring on my own paraplecyc Frech bulldog(Alba), I often faced difficulty to find specific settings where I could leave Alba in case I would need to travel abroad.

I know that keepting a paraplecyc dog, it is not a easy path and requires a full time dedication.
