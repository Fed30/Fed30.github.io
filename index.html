Dog Boarding

We are a high quality kennels that provides a caring, low stress environment for dogs & pets. Our experienced staff will make your pet’s country resort holiday an enjoyable one.

Pet Disabilities

We aim to accommodate your pet whatever his or her needs. Pet with disabilities are welcome. Written instructions needed. Please call to arrange the best service we can offer.

Pet taxi

If you don't have time to drop off your pet, don't panic; we will happily collect your pet in our air-conditioned vehicle. Please call us to request this.

Who we are?

We are a high quality kennels that provides a caring, low stress environment for dogs & pets of most kinds. The last thing you want to worry about whilst on holiday or away from home is the welfare of your beloved pets. We welcome all pets as part of our family and we endeavour to make their stay as comfortable as possible.
